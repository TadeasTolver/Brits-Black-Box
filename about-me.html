About me

I am an audio documentary maker and journalist. The past 20 years I have been working and living between Denmark and Czechia, while producing programmes and podcasts in the three languages I know best: Danish, Czech and English. For years, I worked as an editor for Czech national radio's documentary department. I've also been part of the documentary redaction at the Danish public radio (Radio24syv) as well as with the independent Danish podcast Third Ear. My programs have aired on BBC and ABC. Today I am an independent producer recording audio documentaries, podcasts and investigative series. Occasionally, when somebody needs a hand with a production, I function as editor, dramaturg or script-writer.

If you need something audio, feel free to reach out!
